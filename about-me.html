Ik ben Mathieu

Hello, my name is Maitheu Brugman. I was born and raised in The Hague, Netherlands. During my early 30s I was an art professor for a local college. I loved it at first, but after a while of teaching I realized students were not taking art class to actually learn but to get an easy grade. This made me dislike my work.

I was fortunate enough to be offered a job as an Argricutlural Project Manager from a former colleage of mine. This lead me to lead projects in Sri Lanka, Yemen, and Kenya. I treasure the time and experience I had in Africa and the Middle East.

Enough about me, more about the artwork. Most of what I like to paint are landscapes. However, I paint whatever I feel like. I hope you enjoy what I have to offer.
